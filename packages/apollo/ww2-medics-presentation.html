Who Were the Combat Medics?

The Frontline Heroes

  • Navy Corpsmen served with Marines
  • Army Medics supported infantry units
  • Known simply as "Doc" to their brothers
  • Often unarmed, marked only by red cross
  • First responders under direct enemy fire

The Numbers

  • Medical Corps grew from 40 officers in 1939
  • To over 5,200 medical officers by war's end
  • 4,172 nursing sisters served
  • 40,000+ support personnel
  • Served in every theater of war
