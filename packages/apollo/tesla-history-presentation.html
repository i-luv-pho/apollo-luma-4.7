The History of Tesla

From Silicon Valley Startup to Global EV Leader

"Accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy"

Key Milestones

  • 2003 — Founded by Martin Eberhard & Marc Tarpenning in San Carlos, CA
  • 2004 — Elon Musk invests $6.5M, becomes Chairman & largest shareholder
  • 2008 — Tesla Roadster launches — first highway-legal EV with Li-ion battery
  • 2010 — IPO on NASDAQ, first U.S. automaker IPO since Ford in 1956
  • 2012 — Model S debuts, redefining the luxury EV sedan market
  • 2017 — Model 3 mass production begins, democratizing electric vehicles
  • 2024 — Annual revenue reaches $97.69B, 50%+ U.S. EV market share
